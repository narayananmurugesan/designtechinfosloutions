Virtual Twilight

Make the Magic Hour Work for Your Listings

Twilight real estate photography takes your listing to the next level with striking colors, contrast, and perspectives. Unlike typical daylight photography, twilight photography can only be achieved during a particular window, just as the sun has set. Because of its surreal, sought-after aesthetic, this elusive period of time is known by photographers as the “magic hour.” You can take advantage of the magic hour in Orlando, Daytona Beach, and beyond to highlight your real estate listings with gorgeous lighting, impeccable ambiance, and eye-catching colors unique to twilight on the coast.

Virtual Twilight Terms of Use

The virtual twilight upgrade takes ONE day-time photo and simulates a twilight scene using Photoshop. It is not a replacement for twilight photography with multi-bracketed shots or advanced lighting techniques that will bring in the interior space view. These examples are representative of the kind of lighting and window views you should expect. We do try to remove shadows, add lighting effects and adjust window color to simulate twilight photography. However, if you are expecting to clearly see the inside of the home and have no window reflections, please do not order this option as refunds will not be provided.
